Zeronpa

A Zero Escape fanganronpa

What is Zeronpa ?

Zeronpa is a story centered on characters from the Zero Escape game series (specifically the first two games : 999 and Virtue's Last Reward), going through a Danganronpa-inspired killing game. The rules are simple : every character is locked away in a strange facility, and the only way for them to escape is to commit a murder and get away with it. After a murder has occured, participants will be given some time to investigate, then a trial will take place, to try to unmask the murderer. If the murderer is identified, they will be executed. But if the others fail to identify the murderer, they will be allowed to leave the facility, while everyone else will be executed.

Before you start reading, it is highly recommended to have played the first two Zero Escape games (999 and VLR), as there will be major spoilers for both games, even in the beginning. The third Zero Escape game, Zero Time Dilemma, isn't required though, and neither are any Danganronpa games.
